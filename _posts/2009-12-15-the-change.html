A Promotional Piece

Two versions of New Year's Greetings cards to celebrate the start of 2010. The first version was sent out to prospective clients, friends and family. The second version was sent out to the 'Owl & Lion Gallery' in Edinburgh.

The quote is commonly attributed to Mahatma Gandhi, and is, "We need to be the change we wish to see in the world."
