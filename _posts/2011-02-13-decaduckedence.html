I was asked to create promotional material for a new catering company, called Hunter-Gatherer catering.

The long flyer below acted as an invite and a menu for a catering event. The ten birds featured were all stuffed into each other, starting with the smallest, before being roasted. I also made some flyers for the event's after-party, the 'Deckaduckedance'.
