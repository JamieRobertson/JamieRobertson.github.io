From mid-September to November I worked from the client's office designing and developing a multiple-choice question app for Bright Young Things Tuition.

The app is available for all phones and tablet devices in the Apple and Android stores.

How was it made?

The app was developed with HTML5 & CSS and uses the PhoneGap framework.

- It also uses the jQuery Mobile, touch optimised framework.

- Icons and backgrounds were designed in Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator.

- All programming was done with JavaScript / jQuery.

- The app also uses JSON and the Mandrill API to send a certificate to the user.
