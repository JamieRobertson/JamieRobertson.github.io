I created this graphic identity for a new business consultancy, BYTlab.com.

The consultancy was created by the partnership between 'Bright Young Things Tuition' and 'Group Olivo' consultancy.
To speed up the development process I adapted a Wordpress theme to my needs. I designed a range of identities for the business before settling on a lower-case b inspired by isometric grids and molecular diagrams.
