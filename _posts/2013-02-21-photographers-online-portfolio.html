I made this simple, clean website for a friend of mine who is a great photographer.

The overall concept behind the design is to make the layout as easy as possible to scroll. If the site is viewed from a mobile device orÂ on a portrait format tablet, the images will adjust from a horizontal to a vertical alignment. At a vertical alignment the navigation is hidden to make more space for the photographs.

The site is touch optimised and the sliding panel uses CSS hardware acceleration. I also decided to use Mika Tuupola's jQuery lazyload plugin to give the impression of faster page loading speeds and thus provide a better user experience.
